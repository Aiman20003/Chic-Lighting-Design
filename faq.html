   FREQUENTLY ASKED Question

   Below the most frequently asked questions answer is avilable


1.  The product says 'sold out'. Will you get more & when?

Yes - all products on our website remain in our collection. If an item is shown as 'sold out' we will already have an order underway. We make products available for pre-order as soon as possible. Please ensure you follow our 'new drop' highlights where we update when new stock is available for pre-order.
2. Is there a warranty on the product I buy?

Yes. Manufacturer's warranties vary by manufacturer and product, but we stand behind all of the products we sell and are happy to work with our customers directly to resolve issues relating to product performance.
3. What does pre-order mean

Pre order is when we have stock on the way . If you purchase a pre-order item you are guaranteed one when the stock arrives. Please note we give estimated delivery dates on the product listings. Due to the current situation these are subject to change.
4. How do I know it's safe to use my credit/debit card

We take the protection of your personal and payment information very seriously. Payment information is protected using VeriSign's SSL encryption, so only Bulbs.com can access a purchaser's information. We do not retain or store credit card information after a transaction has been processed.
5. Can I cancel my order?

Please double check all shipping information and product quantities for your order are correct as once an order is placed it will not be possible to cancel or amend prior to dispatch.
6. I have not received my order. Where is it?

t's likely that you have ordered a pre-order item and we are still awaiting the stock. Please check the individual product listing for availability and estimated delivery times. Your order will be on backorder and you will receive an email as soon as your item has been dispatched.
7.  Why are your lead times so long?

Due to the current situation there is a huge amount of shipping congestion worldwide. Consequently it is taking us a lot longer than usual to receive our stock deliveries. Unfortunately at the moment the situation is beyond our control and our availability dates may change resulting in a delay on your items being dispatched. Rest assured we are doing all we can to get your items to you as soon as possible.
8.  Do you ship internationally?

NO. We currently only ship within only some of the countries. We also have a dedicated UK store which can be found here.
